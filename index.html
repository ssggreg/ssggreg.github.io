About Me

I am Gregoire Fournier, a PhD candidate in Mathematics at the University of Illinois Chicago (UIC). My research focuses on AI explainability, Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), and large language models (LLMs). I work on improving model interpretability, ensuring fairness, and bridging AI with legal reasoning.

I am looking for opportunities in data science and AI research where I can apply my expertise in machine learning and model interpretability to solve real-world challenges.
